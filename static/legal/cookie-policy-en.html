PathWave Cookie Policy

This document describes the cookies, local storage, and similar tracking technologies used by the PathWave web consoles (admin-web / provider-web). Our mobile apps (iOS/Android) use OS-standard storage only and are not covered by this cookie policy.

1. Scope

  • Super-admin console (admin-web)
  • Store owner console (provider-web)
  • Static policy pages (this site)

2. Tracking technologies used

TypeNamePurposeRetention
HttpOnly cookie session_id Login session (essential) Until session ends
LocalStorage access_token / refresh_token JWT authentication (essential) Until logout or expiry
LocalStorage i18nextLng Remember language (essential) Until changed
LocalStorage theme / ui-prefs UI preferences (optional) Until changed

3. Ads / analytics tracking

PathWave does not use any ad networks or external analytics trackers (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no ad SDKs).

This is reflected in our iOS Privacy Manifest where NSPrivacyTracking is false.

4. Refusing cookies

  • Refusing essential cookies (session, JWT) makes login impossible.
  • Refusing optional cookies (UI prefs) just resets your preferences each visit.

5. Third-party cookies

When you enter the Toss Payments flow, cookies issued by Toss domains are governed by Toss Payments' own privacy policy.

6. Notice of changes

If this policy changes, you will see a notice when you next access the admin or provider console, and we will announce changes 7 days in advance.
