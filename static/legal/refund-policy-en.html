PathWave Refund Policy

This policy is provided under the Korean Act on the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, the Act on the Regulation of Terms and Conditions, and Apple App Store / Google Play payment policies.

1. Scope

  • Store-owner monthly subscriptions paid directly to PathWave.
  • End-user (mobile app) payments are not offered in v1 and are out of scope.
  • App Store / Google Play in-app purchases follow each store's policy first.

2. General Right of Withdrawal

WhenRefundNote
Within 7 days & service not usedFull refundE-commerce Act §17
Within 7 days & partial useRefund minus pro-rated usage
After 7 daysAuto-renewal cancelled from next cycleCurrent month non-refundable

3. Non-refundable cases

  • Simple change of mind without our fault.
  • Suspension due to terms-of-service breach (e.g. fake business registration). Only the unused portion is pro-rated.
  • Add-on benefits already activated, such as coupons already issued to users.

4. Refunds for outages on our side

If service is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours due to our fault, we credit a pro-rated refund automatically (no separate request needed).

5. How to request a refund

  1. Open provider-web → Billing → Request refund (or email us below).
  2. Include your registered email, business number, and reason.
  3. We respond within 3 business days.
  4. If approved, refund is processed to the original method within 7–10 business days.

Email: billing@pathwave.app

6. Cancelling auto-renewal

You can cancel auto-renewal yourself in the provider-web Billing page up to 24 hours before the next charge. Cancelling does not refund the current cycle — service remains active until the end of the paid period.

7. Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision, you may apply for mediation through the Korea Consumer Agency (☎ 1372) or the Electronic Commerce Dispute Mediation Committee.
