PathWave Privacy Policy

TRIGGER SOFT CO., LTD. (“the Company”) complies with the Personal Information Protection Act, the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization, the Act on the Protection and Use of Location Information, and other applicable laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect & How

CategoryItems

Collection method: entered directly by the user during sign-up and service use, or generated/collected automatically during use.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Member identification, authentication, and fraud prevention
  • BLE beacon-based automatic store services (seamless WiFi connection, stamps, coupons)
  • 1:1 chat between users and stores, including automatic translation
  • Payment, settlement, and refunds
  • Notices and push notifications; marketing information (with separate consent)
  • Service operation/improvement and handling inquiries and disputes

3. Retention Period

In principle, personal information is destroyed without delay once its purpose is achieved or upon account withdrawal. The following are retained as required by law.

ItemPeriodLegal basis
Contracts, withdrawal of subscription, payment & supply records5 yearsE-Commerce Act
Consumer complaint/dispute records3 yearsE-Commerce Act
Advertising/display records6 monthsE-Commerce Act
Login (access) records3 monthsProtection of Communications Secrets Act
Abuse report handling records6 monthsNetwork Act

Upon withdrawal, your own chat messages are anonymized after 30 days; after a 30-day recovery grace period, data other than legally required records is permanently deleted.

4. Provision to Third Parties

The Company does not provide personal information beyond the scope stated in this policy, except: (i) when you give explicit prior consent, or (ii) when required by law or by lawful request of investigative authorities.

6. Processing of Location Information

  • Location data is processed only at the time of service (BLE beacon handshake and store auto-connection).
  • The Company does not perform background (app-not-in-use) location tracking.
  • You may revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time request access, correction, deletion, or suspension of processing, or withdraw consent (account withdrawal). Use Settings > Account in the app, or contact the Privacy Officer below. For deletion without the app, see the Account Deletion page.

8. Destruction Procedure

  • Electronic files: permanently deleted using non-recoverable methods.
  • Printouts: shredded or incinerated.

9. Security Measures

  • One-way encryption of passwords; AES-256 encryption of sensitive data such as payment keys.
  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Minimized access privileges, access control, and retention of access logs.

10. Children Under 14

The Company does not allow sign-up by children under 14 and verifies birth year at sign-up to restrict such accounts.

11. Privacy Officer

  • Privacy Officer: PathWave Privacy Team (Representative: Woosang Lee)
  • Contact: privacy@pathwave.app

12. Remedies

For privacy-related complaints, you may contact Korea’s Personal Information Dispute Mediation Committee (kopico.go.kr, 1833-6972) or the Privacy Infringement Report Center (privacy.kisa.or.kr, 118).

13. Changes

Any additions, deletions, or modifications to this policy will be announced within the service at least 7 days before they take effect.
