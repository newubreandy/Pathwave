PathWave Youth Protection Policy

This policy is provided under the Korean ICT Network Act §42-2 and the Youth Protection Act to protect underage users — including children under 14 — using PathWave.

1. Age 14 Restriction

Birth year is required at sign-up. Users under 14 are blocked.

  • Auto-rejected at sign-up with a "Guardian consent required" notice.
  • Guardian must consent via their own email before the child can register.
  • The age threshold may change if Korean law changes.

2. Guardian Consent Flow

  1. User under 14 attempts to sign up → enters guardian email.
  2. We email the guardian a consent request (valid 24 hours).
  3. Guardian clicks the link and consents → child's account is activated.
  4. If guardian declines, sign-up is blocked.

3. Harmful Content Filtering

  • Alcohol / adult-only stores are tagged (adult_only=1).
  • Adult-only listings are auto-excluded for users under 14.
  • Chat messages with offensive language are flagged automatically. One report triggers immediate review.

4. Extra Protection of Personal Data

  • Data of users under 14 is collected only within the scope of guardian consent.
  • Marketing consent is auto-disabled for users under 14.
  • Location data is used only for BLE beacon detection and is never shared externally.

6. Reporting

If you encounter harmful content or an inappropriate user/store:

  • In-app: open the chat or store, tap ⋮ → "Report" → choose a reason.
  • Email: youth@pathwave.app
